Windows set PHP environment variables

  1. Locate your AMPPS PHP Folder, typically located at C:\Program Files\Ampps\php74 but your PC may vary.
  2. Press START.
  3. In search type in "Edit the system environment variables" then click on it in the list.
  4. Click on the Advanced Tab.
  5. Click on the "Environment Variables" button.
  6. Locate the "system variables" list.
  7. Click on Path.
  8. Click on the Edit button.
  9. Click on the New button.
  10. Type in "C:\Program Files\Ampps\php74" or your own machines equivalent path. Press Enter when finished.
  11. Click on OK, and then click on OK again.
  12. Load CMD under the windows Start Menu.
  13. Type in "php -v" and make sure it is running as PHP 7.4
